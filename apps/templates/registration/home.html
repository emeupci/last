Europe & the Middle East

The Europe and Middle East Region spans the EurAsian Continent; from the picturesque streets of Lisbon in the west to the breathtaking wilderness of Kamchatka in Russia's far east. The divirsity of landscapes is eclipsed only by the diversity of people groups. There is one unifying truth; everyone, everywhere has need of a Savior. This is the mission of the Europe and Middle East United Pentecostal Church: to share the good news of a powerful Savior.
